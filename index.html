Best workflow: choose the slate date, import a projection CSV, then use Minutes Movers, Opponent Factors, and Projection Builder. Your imported database persists in this browser with local storage.
How this works
Core formula: projected stat ≈ projected minutes × player baseline per-minute rate × opponent factor. Minutes movers compare each player's latest slate to their immediately previous slate.

Import rules
Accepted columns can be either the historical database format or raw projection-file format. For raw uploads, the dashboard maps: Minutes, Points, Rebounds, Assists, Steals, Blocks, Three Pointers Made, Turnovers, PRA.
